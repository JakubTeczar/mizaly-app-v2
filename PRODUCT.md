# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Small social-media agency staff and their clients, using the mobile PWA (apps/mobile) and internal admin panel
(apps/admin) day to day to plan, review, and publish content, and to review AI-analyzed inspiration/audit data.
All UI copy is Polish. Users are working, not browsing - every screen serves a concrete task (compose a post,
check what's trending, review a client's audit).

## Product Purpose

Mizaly helps a social media agency plan and publish content across platforms, and surfaces AI-driven insight
(trending posts, hook/format/CTA classification, audience comment segmentation) to inform what to create next.
Success looks like: a user can act on what they see (spot a pattern, get an idea, publish a post) without wading
through noise.

## Brand Personality

Matter-of-fact, dense, no decoration. A working tool, not a showcase - clarity and speed over polish-for-its-own-
sake. The existing purple accent (--color-primary, #694cb8) is the one committed color; everything else stays
restrained neutrals.

## Anti-references

Not a SaaS marketing site. No gradient hero sections, no big illustration-driven empty states, no decorative
glassmorphism. Avoid the "AI dashboard" cliché of big stat tiles and gradient-text numbers - this app's AI
features (classification, segmentation) should read as plain, countable facts, not a flashy analytics product.

## Design Principles

- Density over whitespace: users are working through real data (comments, posts, stats), not admiring a landing page.
- Every AI-derived result must show its basis in plain terms (a count, a literal quote) - never just a vibe.
- Reuse existing tokens/components (.card, .stat-row, .sub-tabs, --color-* variables) before inventing new ones.
- Polish-language copy throughout, consistent with the rest of the app.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No stated formal requirement (no WCAG level specified). Keep interactive elements keyboard-reachable and
color-independent (don't rely on color alone to convey state), consistent with the rest of the existing app.
